# REA Group / PropTrack Property Data APIs

> REA Group Limited (ASX:REA) is a Melbourne-based digital real estate advertising business (operator of realestate.com.au and realcommercial.com.au) majority-owned by News Corp. Its public developer surface is delivered through PropTrack, whose portal documents a suite of Australian property data APIs — address matching, automated valuations (AVM), property and listing data, sold transactions, and suburb-level market insights. The APIs are documented publicly but are partner-gated: access requires a commercial agreement and is authenticated via OAuth 2.0 against the REA Group identity service.

## APIs

- [PropTrack Properties API](https://developer.proptrack.com.au/docs/property-data-apis/properties): Property attributes, sale/listing history, and property reports for a specific property. Base: https://data.proptrack.com/api/v2
- [PropTrack Address API](https://developer.proptrack.com.au/docs/property-data-apis/address): Address match and address suggest resolving free-text/partial addresses to canonical Australian property records (structured address match coming soon).
- [PropTrack Valuations API (AVM)](https://developer.proptrack.com.au/docs/apis/home): Automated Valuation Model estimates and confidence ranges for banking, broking, and valuation workflows.
- [PropTrack Listings API](https://developer.proptrack.com.au/docs/apis/branches/main/listings): Listing details, listing history, listings search by point and radius, and sold-transactions search by point and radius.
- [PropTrack Market API](https://developer.proptrack.com.au/docs/property-data-apis/market): Suburb-level market statistics — sale insights, rent insights, best-value insights, supply & demand, and economic reports.

## Authentication

- [OAuth 2.0](https://developer.proptrack.com.au/docs/apis/authentication): Partner-gated. Credentials issued under a commercial agreement; exchange for a bearer access token used against data.proptrack.com. See authentication/rea-group-authentication.yml.
